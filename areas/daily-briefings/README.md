# Daily Briefings

Prioritized morning briefings generated by `/morning-briefing`. One file per day.

## Structure
- `sessions/YYYY-MM-DD.md` — dated briefing files
- Generated each morning, covering calendar, tasks, 1:1/meeting prep status, signals, and priorities

## What Goes Here
- The synthesized briefing output (calendar, priorities, tasks, signals)
- NOT the 1:1 prep files themselves — those live in `areas/one-on-ones/<person>/sessions/`
- NOT meeting prep — those live in `areas/meetings/<meeting>/sessions/`

## Relationship to Other Areas
The morning briefing triggers 1:1 and meeting prep as a side effect. Session files created during the briefing are stored in their respective person/meeting folders, not here. This folder is the daily "what matters today" snapshot.
